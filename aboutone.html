Who is Wael Abdelwahab?

Wael Abd El-Wahab is an ex architect, graduated from faculty of architecture engineering at Misr International University, he decided to take his career shift since the second year of architecture engineering into the fitness industry.
He competed in more than seven competitions in the men's physique category. AbdEl-Wahab was one of the first physique competitors in Egypt who had great symmetry and a well-conditioned body. After graduating from architecture engineering, Abdelwahab decided to prepare for Mr. Olympia Egypt 2021 while he was serving in the Egyptian Army.
He prepared for one year, and he got first place. His hard work and consistency led him to be one of the best online coaches in the Middle East because of his insane clients' transformations and results, leaving a great fingerprint and changing people’s lives through online coaching.




What's the passion of Captain
Wael AbdEl-Wahab?

Wael AbdEl-Wahab has a great passion for the fitness industry, which made him shift from the architecture field to the fitness field. All of his clients’ stories and everyone he coached influenced him to update and jump to the next level, and it’s still happening.




What value he adds to the fitness industry?

Wael Abd El-Wahab added great value to the fitness industry by changing people’s lives through online coaching, putting all efforts into changing clients mentally before physically. Working as an average online coach wasn’t enough; online coaching became life coaching as well.
